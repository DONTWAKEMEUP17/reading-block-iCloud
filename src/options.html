    Connect iCloud

    Reading Block books straight onto your iCloud calendar. It needs your Apple ID and an app-specific password — not your normal Apple ID password. Create one at appleid.apple.com → Sign-In & Security → App-Specific Passwords. It's stored only in this browser.
